MESSAGE

Thank you for visiting my website. My name is Ryusei Ueno, a sociologist from Japan. My research grows out of a concern that sociology has long been shaped by anthropocentrism. I aim to develop a social theory that allows us to understand humans as they are—always entangled with nonhuman actors and environments.
I hope this site can serve as a small bridge to share these ideas, and I would be delighted to connect with fellow researchers and anyone interested. Please feel free to reach out!
